Our Programs

At Little Sprouts, we offer a variety of programs tailored to meet the developmental needs of children at every stage. Our curriculum is designed to foster growth, learning, and fun in a safe and nurturing environment.

Infant Care

Our Infant Care program provides a warm and stimulating environment for babies aged 6 weeks to 12 months. We focus on creating a secure and loving space where infants can explore, learn, and grow at their own pace. Our caregivers are trained to meet the unique needs of each infant, ensuring they receive personalized attention and care.

Toddler Program

Our Toddler Program is designed for children aged 12 to 24 months. This program focuses on developing toddlers' social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play-based learning. Activities include music, movement, art, and language development, all aimed at fostering independence and curiosity.

Preschool Program

Our Preschool Program is for children aged 2 to 4 years. This program prepares children for kindergarten by focusing on early literacy, math, science, and social studies. We encourage creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork through a variety of engaging activities and projects.
