Enrollment Process

Welcome to Little Sprouts Daycare! We're excited to have you consider us for your child's care and development. Our enrollment process is designed to be straightforward and supportive, ensuring a smooth transition for your family. Please follow the steps below to begin your journey with us.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Submit Application

Complete the online application form with all required information about your child and family.

2. Schedule a Tour

Once your application is received, our team will review it and contact you to schedule a tour of our facility.

3. Attend Tour

Attend the scheduled tour to meet our staff, explore our classrooms, and learn more about our programs.

4. Submit Enrollment Forms

After the tour, if you decide to enroll, you'll need to submit the required enrollment forms and pay the registration fee.

5. Confirmation

Once all forms are submitted and the fee is paid, your child's spot will be confirmed, and we'll provide you with a start date.

Required Forms

Child Information Form

This form provides essential information about your child, including medical history, emergency contacts, and authorized pick-up individuals.

Parent Handbook Agreement

This form outlines our policies and procedures, including payment schedules, attendance, and health guidelines.

Medical Authorization Form

This form authorizes us to administer basic first aid and seek emergency medical care if necessary.

Sunscreen/Insect Repellent Permission

This form allows us to apply sunscreen and insect repellent to your child during outdoor activities.
